Welcome to the TYFTO Series

The "Thank You For This Opportunity" (TYFTO) Talk Series aims to cultivate deeper connection among co-workers beyond their professional roles. This series provides a platform for individuals to share their passion or interests, enhance their presentation and public speaking skills, and create a more connected workplace environment.

Explore the schedule for upcoming talks, find logistical info, browse the gallery, or get in touch with our speakers and organizers.
