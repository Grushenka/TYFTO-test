Welcome to TYFTO

Tell Your Friends Tuesday (TYFTO) is a community-led speaker series that celebrates personal storytelling, skill-sharing, and creative expression. Every Tuesday, a member of our team takes the stage to present something meaningful — from passion projects to life lessons.

Why TYFTO?

TYFTO is more than a series — it's a tradition. It gives our students and staff the opportunity to practice public speaking, learn from each other, and explore topics that matter to them. Whether you're presenting or just attending, you're part of a shared experience that celebrates voice, identity, and creativity.
