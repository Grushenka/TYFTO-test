Logistics Overview

Scheduling

  • The interested speaker contacts the series coordinator (Grushenka).
  • A date will be agreed upon for the talk, and the speaker will be officially scheduled.

Blurb Submission

  • Speakers provide a brief description of their talk as soon as possible for promotion.
  • Deadline: One week prior to the scheduled talk.

Talk Format

  • Each talk is 20 minutes long and delivered in person.
  • Speakers may format their presentation however they prefer.

Q&A

  • After each talk, 10 minutes are allotted for audience questions and engagement.
  • Total session time: 30 minutes.

Topic Guidelines

  • Talks do not need to be work-related.
  • Speakers are encouraged to share personal interests.
