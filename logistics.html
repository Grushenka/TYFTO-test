The Logistics

  • A speaker will present and discuss a topic at each meeting. The talk can be interactive and/or just have a Q&A at the end.
  • Speakers will be given at least 1 month's notice so that they can prepare their talk. They will also provide the coordinator with an abstract closer to the date of their presentation so that everyone can have an idea about the talk in advance.
  • Credit should be given when applicable.
  • Talks are typically held in the basement conference. In the summer, they may be held outside.
  • Resources can be provided to the speaker, if needed.
